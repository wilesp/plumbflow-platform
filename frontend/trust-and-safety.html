Our Verification Process

Every tradesperson on Best Trade goes through a rigorous multi-step verification process before they can accept jobs. We don't cut corners when it comes to your safety.

✓

Identity Verification

Full identity checks including government-issued ID verification and address confirmation to ensure we know exactly who we're working with.

📋

Qualifications Check

We verify all relevant trade qualifications, certifications, and professional memberships. Only properly qualified professionals join our platform.

🛡️

Insurance Confirmation

Public liability insurance is mandatory for all tradespeople. We verify coverage is current and adequate before approval.

🔍

Background Screening

Enhanced DBS checks (where applicable) and professional reference checks ensure you're working with trustworthy professionals.

⭐

Review Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of customer reviews and ratings. Tradespeople with poor feedback are investigated and removed if necessary.

📞

Business Validation

We verify business registration, contact details, and trading history to ensure every professional is legitimate and established.

Your Protection as a Customer

We've built multiple layers of protection to ensure you have a safe, positive experience every time you use Best Trade.

🔒

Secure Platform

All communications and payments are handled through our secure platform. Your personal details are never shared without your permission.

💳

No Payment Until Complete

You only pay the tradesperson directly once the work is completed to your satisfaction. No upfront fees, no hidden charges.

📝

Written Quotes

All quotes are provided in writing through the platform, creating a clear record of what was agreed before work begins.

🆘

Dispute Resolution

If something goes wrong, our customer service team is here to help. We mediate disputes and work to find fair resolutions.

⚠️

Report System

Easily report any concerns about a tradesperson's conduct, quality of work, or professionalism. We investigate all reports promptly.
