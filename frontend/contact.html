Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job as a customer?
Simply click "Post a Job" on our homepage, fill in the details about what you need done, and we'll instantly match you with qualified local tradespeople. It's completely free and takes less than 2 minutes!
How much does it cost for tradespeople to join?
We offer three subscription tiers: Basic (£20/month), Pro (£30/month), and Premium (£200/year). Each tier offers different benefits including lead volume, coverage area, and priority placement. Visit our Pricing page for full details.
Are all tradespeople verified?
Yes, absolutely. Every tradesperson goes through our rigorous verification process including identity checks, qualification verification, insurance confirmation, and background screening. We never compromise on safety and quality.
How quickly will I get quotes?
Most customers receive their first quote within an hour, and typically have 3-5 quotes within 24 hours. For emergency jobs, responses are often even faster as tradespeople receive instant SMS notifications.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
We take quality seriously. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team and we'll help mediate. We monitor all customer reviews and investigate any complaints to ensure high standards across the platform.
Can I cancel my tradesperson subscription?
Yes, you can cancel at any time with no penalties. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. We want you to stay because you love the service, not because you're locked in!
What areas do you cover?
We currently cover the entire UK with tradespeople in all major cities and towns. Our Premium tier offers nationwide coverage, while Basic and Pro tiers focus on local area coverage (10-20 miles).
How do payments work?
Customers pay the tradesperson directly once the work is completed to their satisfaction - we don't take any commission. Tradespeople pay their monthly subscription fee which is processed automatically via card payment.
