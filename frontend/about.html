Our Story

Best Trade was founded in 2024 with a simple idea: finding a reliable tradesperson shouldn't be stressful. We've all been there – searching endlessly online, not knowing who to trust, waiting days for quotes that never come.

We built this marketplace to solve that problem. By carefully vetting every tradesperson and using smart matching technology, we connect homeowners with the right professional for their job – quickly, reliably, and without the hassle.

What started as a focused service has grown into the UK's leading all-trades marketplace. Today, we help thousands of homeowners across the UK find trusted plumbers, electricians, builders, decorators, gardeners, and more – all in one place.
