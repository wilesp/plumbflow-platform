Our Story

Best Trade was founded in 2024 with a simple idea: finding a reliable tradesperson shouldn't be stressful. We've all been there – searching endlessly online, not knowing who to trust, waiting days for quotes that never come.

We built this marketplace to solve that problem. By carefully vetting every tradesperson and using smart matching technology, we connect homeowners with the right professional for their job – quickly, reliably, and without the hassle.

What started as a focused service has grown into the UK's leading all-trades marketplace. Today, we help thousands of homeowners across the UK find trusted plumbers, electricians, builders, decorators, gardeners, and more – all in one place.

How We're Different

No Bidding Wars

Unlike other platforms, tradespeople don't compete in a race to the bottom. They send genuine quotes based on fair pricing for quality work. This means better service for homeowners and sustainable income for professionals.

Quality Over Quantity

We carefully vet every professional before they join. Background checks, qualification verification, and review monitoring ensure you get the best. We'd rather have 1,000 excellent tradespeople than 10,000 mediocre ones.

Fair for Tradespeople

We believe in treating tradespeople fairly. Our subscription model means they get reliable lead flow without excessive fees eating into their earnings. Happy tradespeople provide better service to customers.

Local Focus

We match you with tradespeople in your area who can respond quickly and understand your local needs. No more waiting weeks for someone to drive from the other side of the country.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no commission on quotes, no surprise charges. Homeowners post for free, tradespeople pay a simple monthly subscription. Everyone knows exactly what they're getting.

Real Verification

We don't just check a box. Every tradesperson is verified with insurance confirmation, qualification checks, and identity verification. Your safety and peace of mind matter to us.
